Industries Served

As a program management software provider for facility owners, e-Builder serves many organizations to provide cloud-based construction project planning across multiple industries.

Read White Paper

Manage projects
from start to finish

e-Builder is specifically designed to manage multi-project construction programs from start to finish. From planning and design through procurement, construction and operations, e-Builder provides enterprise-wide, web-based construction project planning and management solutions.

e-Builder fits each organization’s individual needs for cost control, collaboration, visibility and transparency.

Request a Demo

Why e-Builder?

Whether owner or stakeholder, e-Builder delivers:

  • Increased project quality and delivery
  • Accurate forecasts to avoid costly surprises
  • On-demand performance dashboards put information at your fingertips
  • Process consistency through automated workflows
  • Reduced risk through improved visibility and contingency management
  • Real-time collaboration between owner, designers, contractors & suppliers
  • Anywhere, anytime access across all platforms including mobile devices
